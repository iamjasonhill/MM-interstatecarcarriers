# Interstate Car Carriers Visual Direction

This repo owns the public subdomain brand direction for `quoting.interstatecarcarriers.com.au`.

## Source Read

The current site presents Interstate Car Carriers as a national vehicle transport service for standard, prestige, enclosed, specialist, and non-drivable vehicle movements. The homepage uses a dark transport-control-room shell, steel-blue CTAs, compact corners, uppercase condensed display headings, and dispatch-board or carrier-lane motifs.

## Brand Choices

- Use a dark charcoal header, footer, and optional hero shell to carry the main site identity into Moveroo.
- Keep the quote form body light, fast, and uncluttered so the first form step is quickly reachable on mobile.
- Use steel blue as the primary action color. Warm amber can appear only as a small secondary signal, not as the main CTA color.
- Use `Barlow Condensed` style uppercase headings with `Inter` style body copy.
- Use the text mark `ICC` when a compact mark is needed. Do not invent a crest or detailed vehicle logo.
- Prefer route, lane, dispatch-board, and car-carrier language over generic moving-truck or house-removal imagery.

## Avoid

- Do not copy Backloading Removals black/red treatment.
- Do not turn the public quote app into a fully dark form experience.
- Do not add provider, admin, or implementation links to public navigation.
- Do not make the first quote step sit below a large marketing block on mobile.

## Moveroo Adaptation Notes

Moveroo should keep its shared subdomain layout and navigation model, then apply this repo's tokens for typography, color, shell treatment, CTA styling, compact mark, and copy. Public contact buttons should use the shared Moveroo phone and email details supplied in the handoff JSON.
