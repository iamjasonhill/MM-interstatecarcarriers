# Interstate Car Carriers Homepage Audit

Last updated: 2026-04-19

## Goal Of This Pass

Create a stronger homepage shell than the current WordPress front page without pretending the route migration is already solved.

## Direction Chosen

- premium interstate vehicle transport look and feel
- stronger hero composition rather than generic stacked blocks
- direct quote-led handoff
- language focused on vehicle transport, enclosed transport, and specialist handling
- fewer filler claims and no fake testimonial copy

## What The New Homepage Covers

- national vehicle transport positioning
- clear quote CTA
- clear contact CTA
- service summary
- route corridor examples
- reasons to choose the brand

## What It Deliberately Does Not Pretend To Cover Yet

- full WordPress route replacement
- full legal/informational page set
- final trust proof from migrated reviews or review widgets
- production redirect behavior
