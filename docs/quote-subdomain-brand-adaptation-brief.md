# Quote Subdomain Brand Adaptation Brief

## Scope

Apply the Interstate Car Carriers brand to Moveroo public quote, booking, contact, and customer portal entry surfaces for:

- `quoting.interstatecarcarriers.com.au`

The source of truth for the machine-readable import is `brand/moveroo-subdomain.json`.

## Standard Routes

- Vehicle quote: `/quote/vehicle`
- Household quote: `/quote/household`
- Booking: `/booking/create`
- Contact: `/contact`
- Customer login: `/customer/login`

## Navigation Intent

Use the same generic Moveroo subdomain layout and nav structure as other imported subdomains. Make vehicle quote the primary action because this site is vehicle-transport led. Keep household quote, booking, contact, and customer login available through the standard relative links without making them visually compete with the first vehicle quote step.

Do not show provider login or admin links on public Interstate Car Carriers quote surfaces.

## Copy Direction

Primary promise: interstate vehicle transport without the guesswork.

Support the following service context:

- Open and enclosed car transport.
- Standard, prestige, specialist, and non-drivable vehicle handling.
- Australia-wide lane and route coordination.
- Quote-first flow for standard jobs, contact path for higher-touch jobs.

## Visual Direction

Use a dark steel transport shell for the header, footer, and optional compact hero. Use light form panels, crisp borders, steel-blue CTA states, and compact radii. The form should feel operational and fast, not like a long marketing landing page.

## Mobile Guardrail

On mobile, keep the first form field reachable quickly. The branded hero may be dark, but it should be compact and should not push the first quote step below the initial viewport.
